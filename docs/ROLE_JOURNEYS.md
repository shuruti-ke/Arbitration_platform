# Role Journey Checklist

## Claimant / Party

- Log in and land on assigned case summary.
- Submit or view case materials.
- Upload permitted documents.
- View hearings and join scheduled sessions.
- Review awards, notices, and payment status where applicable.

## Counsel

- Access only represented party cases.
- Upload pleadings, evidence, and correspondence.
- Review hearing links, milestones, and procedural directions.
- Use court filing and compliance checks for permitted matters.

## Arbitrator

- Review assigned cases and conflict information.
- Accept or decline appointments.
- View documents and hearing schedule.
- Draft or package awards where authorized.
- Upload payment proof and view payment status.

## Secretariat

- Intake and triage new cases.
- Manage parties, counsel, documents, and hearings.
- Support signing readiness and compliance workflows.
- Monitor operational readiness through the admin team.

## Admin

- Manage users, roles, assignments, settings, and payments.
- Review analytics, intelligence, audit logs, and operator status.
- Run deployment verification and incident checks.
- Coordinate backup, restore, and rollback procedures.
